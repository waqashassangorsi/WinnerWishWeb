Staff

Adding Staff Members

In Swatch theme its super easy to add and showcase the staff members of your company.

Staff Pages

Staff Members

  1. Staff name.
  2. Staff info. Everything before the ‘more’ tag will appear as intro content in the staff shortcode. The content after the ‘more’ tag will appear in the single staff page.
  3. Group the staff members in existing departments or add new departments on the spot.
  4. Enter the order of the staff.
  5. Add a feature image (portrait) of the staff.
  6. Select the color swatch of the staff member.
  7. Select where you want the link on the staff list to take you (options are “default link” that will redirect you to the single service page, “page”, “post”, “portfolio”, “category” and “url”). In that way the link when you click on the staff name you can link to anything you wish.
  8. Add the personal details. Position in the company, moto title and text (they appear on hover).
  9. Select the social options of the staff member.
